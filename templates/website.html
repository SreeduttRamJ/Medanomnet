About MedAnomNet

Our hybrid deep learning model is designed to assist medical professionals in detecting anomalies in various medical imaging formats.

Model Architecture

MedAnomNet uses a hybrid pipeline that includes preprocessing (resizing, normalization, edge detection), followed by feature extraction using a frozen ResNet-50. Features from intermediate layers are fused and passed to an adversarial autoencoder for anomaly detection. A classifier parallelly predicts disease types. Heatmaps highlight abnormal regions, aiding medical professionals in decision-making.
